Trouble Shooting.

Trouble shooting involves finding out a problem , defining the problem and looking for possible solution to solve the problem.

Steps Of Trouble Shooting,

      Define the problem.
      -Collecting relevantinformation of the problem.
      -Analyze the information.
      -Enough information to propose a solution.
      -Propose solution and test it.
      -If you solution do not fix the problem iterate to analyze information.
      -If the idea fix the problem implement it.
